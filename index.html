The Craft

From Forest Floor
to Barrel

Every bottle of Ancient Proof begins in the shaded hollows of Appalachia, where wild American ginseng grows slowly beneath the canopy — sometimes taking decades to mature. The root is harvested by hand using techniques passed down through generations of Kentucky hunters.

Then, modern science takes over. Our proprietary hi-tech extraction process captures the full spectrum of ginsenosides — the active compounds that make ginseng legendary — while harmonizing perfectly with aged Kentucky straight bourbon. Ancient secrets. Modern precision. No shortcuts. No synthetics. Just the root and the proof.

Our Heritage

Rooted in the Hills

Roots

The Ancient Hunters

For centuries, American ginseng hunters walked the Appalachian hills with mattocks and burlap sacks — guided by secret techniques passed from father to son. Where to look, how to read the forest, when the root is ready. Knowledge that can't be taught in a classroom.

Land

Basham Farms, Kentucky

On the fertile rolling hills of rural Kentucky, where the soil runs deep and the hollows stay cool, the perfect conditions for wild American ginseng have existed for centuries. Three generations of the family have worked this land.
