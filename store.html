How to Drink It

Pour It Your Way

There's no wrong way to drink Ancient Proof. But here are a few ways the people who make it prefer it.

The Digger's Pour

The Way
  • 2 oz Ancient Proof Original Blend
  • One large ice cube
  • Nothing else

Pour it slow. Let the ice open it up for a minute. Smell it before you sip it. That earthy warmth is the ginseng talking. This is how the hunters drink it after a long day in the hollers.

Holler Water

Original
  • 2 oz Ancient Proof Original Blend
  • Local wildflower honey to taste
  • Splash of branch water
  • Lemon wheel

Stir honey into a splash of warm water until dissolved. Add Ancient Proof and cold branch water over ice. Drop in the lemon. Simple as the land it came from.

Root & Smoke

Bold
  • 2 oz Ancient Proof Barrel Select
  • 0.5 oz real maple syrup
  • 2 dashes walnut bitters
  • Smoked orange peel

Stir everything with ice for 30 seconds. Strain over a large rock. Torch the orange peel over the glass — let the oils catch fire for half a second. Drop it in. Walk away from the bar slowly.

Porch Sipper

Easy
  • 1.5 oz Ancient Proof Original Blend
  • Ginger ale
  • Fresh mint from the yard
  • Lime wedge

Tall glass. Ice to the top. Pour Ancient Proof, fill with ginger ale. Slap the mint between your hands to wake it up. Squeeze the lime and drop it in. Best served on a creaky porch at dusk.
