The Root

The Power of
Wild Ginseng

For centuries, American ginseng hunters have walked the Appalachian hollers in a quiet ritual — reading the land, finding the prong, unearthing the root with bare hands and a mattock. This isn't farming. It's an art form, passed down through generations of families who know the mountains like the lines on their own palms.

Wild American ginseng (Panax quinquefolius) is one of the most valued botanicals on earth. Rich in ginsenosides — the active compounds that support natural energy, cognitive clarity, and immune function — it's the cornerstone of Ancient Proof.

American Harvested.
Sustainably Sourced.

Every root used in Ancient Proof is American harvested — wild-dug from the hills of Appalachia using sustainable practices that ensure the ginseng population continues to thrive. We follow strict harvesting guidelines, planting seeds for every root taken.

This isn't about volume. It's about quality. The same patient respect that guided hunters for centuries guides us today.

The Craft

From Forest Floor
to Barrel

Every bottle of Ancient Proof begins in the shaded hollows of Appalachia, where wild American ginseng grows slowly beneath the canopy — sometimes taking decades to mature. The root is harvested by hand using techniques passed down through generations of Kentucky hunters.

Then, modern science takes over. Our proprietary hi-tech extraction process captures the full spectrum of ginsenosides — the active compounds that make ginseng legendary — while harmonizing perfectly with aged Kentucky straight bourbon.

No shortcuts. No synthetics. Just the root and the proof.
